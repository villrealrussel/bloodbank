Blood Bank:

Dugtong Buhay Program

The Blood Bank: Dugtong Buhay Program is a movement dedicated to ensuring that the most vulnerable people have access to enough, safe, and high-quality blood. The Dugtong Buhay Program, in collaboration with other blood service facilities around the country, will be the country's primary provider of blood and blood products. In order to achieve adequacy, we continue to promote voluntary non-remunerated blood donation.
